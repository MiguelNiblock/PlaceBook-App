HOW WE COLLECT AND USE YOUR INFORMATION

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways.  The information we may collect via the Application depends on how you use the services, and includes:  

 

Location Information

We only collect the data that you explicitly provide to us if you sign up for an account. Otherwise, the Application will operate in offline mode and no data will be collected by us. If you decide to sign up, the specific types of data we collect are:

  1. 1.Data you attach to the Places you save, such as Place’ name, address, coordinates, description, list name, etc. 

  2. 2.Data you attach to the Lists you create, such as List’ name, icon & color. 

  3. 3.Your username and password. Passwords are encrypted by our servers before being stored in our database. 

 

This data is collected only for the purpose of allowing you to access your places on multiple devices. We will not use, analyze, share or sell this data in any other way.

Application Permissions

The Application will request permission to access your device location, only while the Application is running. The purpose of this is so the Application can show you your current location in the map, and tell you your current address when the Application starts. This is not a requirement to use the rest of the Application. If you wish to change these permissions, you may do so in your device’s settings. We do not collect your device location automatically. We only collect location information when you save places to an existing account, although our third party services might collect information automatically. (See section: “COLLECTION AND USAGE OF YOUR INFORMATION BY THIRD PARTIES”)

COLLECTION AND USAGE OF YOUR INFORMATION BY THIRD PARTIES

The Application utilizes various third party services in order to provide you with our services. These third party services have their own privacy policies which are passed down to you, the end user.

 

Google Maps SDK for Android

The map functionality is powered by Google’s Maps SDK for Android. To provide their services through our Application, Google collects and receives data from us and you, including search terms, IP addresses, and latitude/longitude coordinates. Google and its affiliates may use and retain this data to provide and improve Google products and services, subject to the Google Privacy Policy at https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/.

 

The data Google collects about you is sent directly to Google through the normal functioning of the Google Maps Core Services, and we do not have access to that information, including data of any European end user. (where “European” means “European Economic Area, Switzerland, or the UK”) The complete privacy policy of the Maps SDK for Android can be found at https://cloud.google.com/maps-platform/terms/.

 

The information Google collects about you through the use of the Application only includes that which can be automatically inferred from your interactions with the map, such as your current location and coordinates of the locations you pressed. On the other hand, when you save Places, Lists, addresses, etc., these features are beyond the scope of the Maps SDK, and therefore these items are not stored by Google.

 

Expo Services

The Application utilizes Expo’s managed workflow for development purposes. By default, all Expo apps contain code for requesting the Android Advertising ID.

 

The Application utilizes Expo’s Location API for reverse geo-coding. This is necessary in order to provide you with the address of a location when you long-press the map.

 

These types of information are subject to Expo’s privacy policy, which can be found at https://docs.expo.io/regulatory-compliance/data-and-privacy-protection/.
